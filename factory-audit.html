Factory Audit

Transparency and quality assurance at every step

We welcome prospective partners, distributors, and OEM clients to audit our manufacturing facilities. Whether on-site or via live video conference, we provide full visibility into our production processes, quality control systems, and compliance practices.

Audit Options

On-Site Audit

Visit our manufacturing facility in person. Walk the production floor, inspect quality control stations, review documentation, and meet with our engineering and operations teams.

Virtual Audit

Schedule a live video conference walkthrough of our factory. Our team will guide you through each production stage, answer questions in real time, and share relevant documentation on screen.

What the Audit Covers

  • Production Lines: SMT assembly, manual assembly stations, and final product integration processes.
  • Quality Control: Incoming material inspection (IQC), in-process quality control (IPQC), and outgoing quality assurance (OQA) procedures.
  • Testing & Certification: Environmental testing chambers, electrical safety testing, and functional validation equipment.
  • Documentation: ISO-compliant process documentation, traceability records, and compliance certifications.
  • Supply Chain: Key component sourcing, vendor qualification records, and material traceability.

Preparation & Scheduling

To ensure a productive audit experience, we recommend:

  • Scheduling at least 5 business days in advance for on-site visits.
  • Providing your audit checklist or specific focus areas prior to the visit.
  • Virtual audits can typically be arranged within 48 hours.
  • Our team will prepare relevant documentation and samples based on your areas of interest.

Confidentiality

We understand the sensitive nature of manufacturing and business information. All audit participants will be asked to sign a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) prior to the audit. Any proprietary processes or client-specific information observed during the audit shall remain confidential.
